New Zealand Rental Cars

New Zealand offers a huge variety of rental cars options. Travelling by car is one the best ways to explore the beauty of New Zealand because it allows you the freedom of traveling to the most remote places in the country. We recommend booking your hire car many months in advance (especially between November and March) as finding available cars for hire in New Zealand at the last minute can be difficult. Christchurch and Auckland airport have a large number of rental car companies nearby - many of them offer free airport transfers.

Shoestring Rentals

A Christchurch airport car rental company offering everything from small cars to people movers, free 24/7 airport & hotel pickups. Lonely Planet recommended.

Airport Rentals

Christchurch Airport based rentals offering a range of cars, 4WD wagons, minibuses, people-movers and vans. Free airport/hotel transfers, no extra fees.

James Blond

Offers a range of quality rental cars available 24/7 from either Auckland airport or their city base. Also offers a Hotel delivery service if required.

Snap Rentals

Offers a large selection of modern vehicles, cheap airport transfers, roadside assist, unlimited drivers with locations in Auckland and Christchurch.

Jucy Rentals

Offers a range of rental cars, a 24 hour call centre, free transfers from Airports / nearby hotels, plus complimentary touring guides and maps.

Ace Rental Cars

Offers a large selection of rental cars, 12-seater minibuses and people-movers. Boasts unlimited KMs, roadside assistance and complimentary shuttles.

Avis Rental Cars

Offers a wide range of rental cars throughout New Zealand. All vehicles come with 24 hour roadside assistance plus unlimited kilometres / miles.

About NZ Rental Cars

Offers a large fleet of modern rental cars, vans for self-drive holidays and sporting groups. Boasts competitive rates and branches located throughout NZ.

Apex Rentals

Offers a large fleet of late model rental cars boasting unlimited kilometres and free roadside assistance. GPS, ski rack and snow chain hire also available.

NZ Airport Rentals

Offers a New Zealand rental car brand comparison facility, to help find deals on economic runarounds, SUVs, 4WDs, Station Wagons and vans.

Budget Rent A Car

Offers over 40 car rental locations nationwide, including in-terminal car hire at Auckland, Rotorua, Wellington, Christchurch and Queenstown Airports.

Omega Rental Cars

Provides a range of modern NZ rental cars at competitive unlimited km rates with free Roadside Assistance and no extra fees for additional drivers.
