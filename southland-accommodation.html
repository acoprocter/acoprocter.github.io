Catlins and Invercargill Accommodation

There are a many excellent accommodation options in Invercargill. For quality hotels in the centre of the city, Kelvin Hotel is a good option. For motels, we recommend either Homestead Villa or Balmoral Lodge Motel. If you are looking for apartments, Quest Invercargill offers quality lodgings. For bed and breakfasts near the city, look no further than Beersheba and Tudor Park Country Stay. Invercargill Top 10 Holiday Park offers quality holiday accommodation. While visiting the Catlins, we recommend staying at McLean Falls Holiday Park.

Invercargill Accommodation

Kelvin Hotel

Centrally located Invercargill accommodation offering tastefully decorated hotel rooms, superior suites and two bedroom apartments.

Quest Invercargill

Offers modern, 5 star rated, self contained and serviced studio, 1 and 2 bedroom apartments located in the centre of Invercargill.

Invercargill Top 10 Holiday Park

Modern holiday accommodation situated in quiet rural surroundings near Invercargill, offering Cabins, motels, powered campervan and non-powered tenting sites.

Beersheba Bed and Breakfast

Offers luxury, boutique accommodation featuring leather suites, quality furnishings / linens located on 4 acres of woodland gardens near Invercargill.

Homestead Villa Motel

Centrally located Invercargill Motel accomodation offering quality self-contained suites, studios, one and two bedroom units with kitchen facilities.

Balmoral Lodge Motel

Offers four star plus motel accommodation in central Invercargill. Choose from a range of quiet and secure, fully self contained units.

Tudor Park Country Stay

Offers quality lodgings with private facilities in a tranquil garden setting 20 minutes from Invercargill. An ideal base for exploring Southland.

Catlins Accommodation

McLean Falls Eco Holiday Park

Offers a range of self contained Catlins holiday accommodation featuring modern bathroom facilities and an underfloor heated laundry / kitchen area.
