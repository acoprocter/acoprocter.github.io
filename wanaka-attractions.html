Wanaka Attractions

Wanaka offers many exciting and memorable tourist attractions for you to choose from. One of the best ways to take in the beauty of Lake Wanaka and the Mountain Aspiring National Park is on the many walkways that are located beside the lake. An excellent short walk (1 hour return) is up Mt Iron. The terrain is easy to negotiate and the views are unforgettable. Another more challenging walk, is up Mt Roy on the western side of the Lake. Other tourist attractions include mountain climbing, river kayaking, water skiing, snow skiing, skydiving and scenic flights.

Skydive Lake Wanaka

Offers tandem skydiving from 12,000 or 15,000 feet over Lake Wanaka and the spectacular Mount Aspiring National Park.

Puzzling World

Located 2 minutes from Wanaka township and offering a "modern-styled" Great Maze. Also features Illusion Rooms and a cafe.

Alpine Fishing Guides

Offers guided drift-fishing and spin fishing experiences on the many world-renowned fly-fishing rivers near Lake Wanaka and Central Otago.

Adventure Consultants

Offers climbing courses, guided ascents, trekking throughout the Southern Alps including Mt Aspiring, Mount Cook, the Bonar Glacier and Mt French.

New Zealand Wild Walks

Offers small group guided hiking / climbing adventures in the Mount Aspiring National Park and South West World Heritage area.

Alpine Kayak Guides

Offers guided kayaking trips on local Wanaka Rivers including the mighty Clutha river, Makarora river and the glacial fed Matukituki river.

Treble Cone Skifield

Offers the largest skiable terrain, the longest groomed trails, the highest vertical rise in the South Island and spectacular Lake views.

Cardrona Alpine Resort

A popular, family-friendly skifield 30 minutes from Wanaka offering excellent natural snow due to a higher elevation and southerly aspect.

Aspiring Mountain Guides

Specialises in custom guided ascents of New Zealand's highest peaks including Mt Cook, Mt Aspiring, Tasman, Tutoko and MalteBrun.

Lake Wanaka Tourism

The official tourism guide to visiting Lake Wanaka including information on the resort's attractions, accommodation, upcoming events and more.
