Queenstown Accommodation

Queenstown offers a huge variety of accommodation options to choose from. Whether you are looking for self-contained Motels or Apartments in the central township or luxurious Resorts with Lodge style facilities, you will find the ideal place to stay. If you are searching for Lakefront apartments in Queenstown or hotels a short walk from the centre, we have a comprehensive listing of providers. We also list several luxury holiday homes and houses (up to five bedrooms in size).

Pounamu Apartments

Offers luxury studio, one and two bedroom apartments featuring free broadband Internet access plus stunning lake / mountain views.

The Glebe Apartments

Offers luxury Queenstown apartments featuring designer kitchens, spacious contemporary living with decks and balconies that boast superb mountain views.

Hotel St Moritz Queenstown

Offers centrally located, luxury accommodation boasting well-appointed rooms, a Restaurant, Bar, Library, gym, hot tub garden and superb views.

Novotel Queenstown Lakeside

Offers Queenstown's most centrally located 4-star hotel, featuring spacious rooms, an in-house restaurant, WIFI access and a spa pool.

Mercure Queenstown Resort

Offers quality hotel accommodation boasting rooms with lake views plus facilities including a pool, sauna, gymnasium and in-house Restaurant.

Sofitel Queenstown Hotel & Spa

Offers centrally located, luxury Queenstown accommodation boasting elegantly appointed rooms plus a Restaurant, guest lounge, Spa and Cafe.

Views On Tussock

Luxury 3-bedroom villa accommodation boasting a spacious open plan kitchen, outdoor hot tub, private courtyard, free WIFI and spectacular lake views from every room.

Highview Apartments

Offers well-appointed one and two bedroom apartments, architecturally designed with excellent views of Lake Wakatipu and the Remarkables mountain range.
