Wellington Accommodation

The capital city offers a huge variety of accommodation options to choose from. Whether you are looking for centrally located Hotels in Wellington (just a few minutes walk to most of the city's main attractions), fully serviced central city Apartments with harbour views, you will find the ideal accomodation here. We also list Bed and Breakfast accommodation (Austinvilla b & b) for those who enjoy hosted lodgings - which is located just minutes from Wellington's main shopping area.

Offers centrally located apartment accommodation featuring large kitchens, open plan lounge / dining area and tiled balconies.
