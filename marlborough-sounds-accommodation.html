Marlborough Sounds Accommodation

Picton and the Marlborough Sounds offer plenty of accommodation options for all budgets. In Picton, we recommend staying at either Jasmine Court Motel, Harbour View Motel or Picton Holiday Park. In the Marlborough Sounds, see below for a range of highly recommended Resorts, Lodges, B&B's and holiday houses that offer a memorable stay in some of the most beautiful and remote parts of the Sounds.

Picton | Marlborough Sounds

Picton Accommodation

ASURE Jasmine Court Motel

Offers quality, 4 star plus motel accommodation in a quiet, central location. Boasts spacious rooms, off-street parking and free wireless Internet.

Harbour View Motel

Offers spacious motel accommodation boasting ensuite bathrooms, flat screen TVs, stunning harbour views and a central location.

Marlborough Sounds Accommodation

Punga Cove

Nestled amongst native bush in remote Queen Charlotte Sound, offering a range of accommodation including chalets, luxury apartments and budget lodgings.

Bay of Many Coves Resort

Located 30 minutes by water taxi from Picton and offering luxury hillside, apartment lodgings boasting uninterrupted Queen Charotte Sound views.

Ngaio Bay Bed and Breakfast

Offers peaceful, sunny and private cottage-style accomodation overlooking a secluded beach in a remote part of the Marlborough Sounds.

Peppers Portage

Set on the Kenepuru Sound waters edge and offering everything from quality suites with large decks to family rooms boasting stunning sea views.

Lochmara Lodge

An eco-retreat set on the waters edge amongst 11 acres of native bush offering accommodation plus a Wildlife Recovery and Arts Centre.
