Nelson and Nelson Lakes Accommodation

Nelson offers many accommodation options including Apartments, Hotels and Motels in the city centre. If you are looking for bed and breakfast accommodation, we recommend Wakefield Quay House. For family holiday lodgings, we recommend staying at Richmond Top 10 Holiday Park. In the Nelson Lakes region, Owen River Lodge offers a stunning location next to the Owen River.

Nelson Accommodation

DeLorenzo's Motel Apartments

Offers luxuriously appointed apartments in central nelson featuring super king beds, ensuite bathrooms, spa baths and a barbeque area.

Cable Bay Nest

Offers an architecturally designed, fully self-contained, eco-friendly chalet boasting a luxurious bathroom, private balcony and picturesque Cable Bay views.

Trailways Hotel

Offers centrally located Hotel accommodation featuring a swimming pool plus a restaurant / bar overlooking the Maitai river.

Walmer Apartment

Offers spacious apartment accommodation on the top storey of a large, colonial home featuring magnificent Tasman bay views.

Harbour View Apartments

Offers luxury one and two bedroom apartments in Nelson featuring open plan living, stylish decor and breathtaking harbour views.

Wakefield Quay House

A heritage villa offering luxury bed and breakfast accommodation located just metres from the sea overlooking Tasman Bay and the harbour entrance.

Richmond Accommodation

Gladstone Motel

Offers well appointed Richmond Motel accommodation featuring self-contained 1 and 2 bedroom units with super king beds and broadband Internet.

Nelson Lakes Accommodation

Owen River Lodge

Offers luxury, 5 star boutique accommodation near the Owen river. Enjoy fine food, superb service and outstanding fishing nearby.

Alpine Lodge - St Arnaud

Offers well appointed lodge accommodation at the edge of the Nelson Lakes National Park. Features open plan living and underfloor heating.
