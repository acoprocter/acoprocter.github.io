Auckland Attractions

We recommend visiting the Auckland Sky Tower where you can enjoy spectacular views of the city from hundreds of metres up. Other popular tourist attractions include the Auckland Museum, Kelly Tarlton's Underwater World, Auckland Zoo and the Sky Tower Sky Jump. We also highly recommend visiting the many Islands east of Auckland - particularly Rangitoto Island, Waiheke Island and the rugged, untouched Great Barrier Island. If a walk through native rainforest and along a scenic coastline interests you, then the Waitakere Ranges Regional Park is well worth visiting.

SKYCITY Sky Tower and Casino

Located in the heart of Auckland's CBD, offering spectacular panoramic city views, an exhilarating Sky jump, a busy casino area, Hotel accommodation and more.

Bush and Beach Tours

Offers informative, interactive guided eco-tours of the city and greater Auckland region. See lush coastal rainforest, black sand beaches, Great Barrier Island and more.

Auckland War Memorial Museum

Showcases the natural, cultural and social history of New Zealand. Also offers informative, fascinating guided tours and entertaining Maori cultural performances.

Kelly Tarlton's SEA LIFE Aquarium

Showcases over 30 live animal exhibits including the world's largest Antarctic penguin colony exhibit. See also deep-sea Sharks, giant Stingrays, seahorses and more.

Great Barrier Island

Visitors guide to the remote, tranquil and untouched paradise of Great Barrier Island, one of New Zealand's finest eco-tourism retreats, located in the Hauraki Gulf.

Auckland Zoo

Home to the largest collection of native and exotic animals in New Zealand. See giraffes, lions, hippos, penguins, tigers, the native kiwi, tuatara and more.

Waiheke Island

Online guide to visiting the tropical island paradise, Waiheke Island, including information on transport options, accommodation, tourist attractions and more.

Auckland Explorer Bus

Offers hop on, hop off sightseeing bus tours that visit Auckland's main attractions including Kelly Tarltons, the Auckland Museum, Skytower and Parnell Village.

Waitakere Ranges

A large native rainforest and stunning coastline offering many walking tracks that allow you to explore the rugged beauty of the wild west coast.
