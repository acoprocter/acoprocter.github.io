Oamaru Accommodation

Oamaru offers a variety of accommodation options. For Motel lodgings, we recommend staying at Oamaru Motor Lodge or Avenue Motel. For those of you in search of hosted lodgings, look no further than Highway House Bed and Breakfast. To experience the beauty of the North Otago high country, we recommend staying at either the Country Lodge at Dome Hills Station or Glenmac Farmstay.

Glenmac Farmstay

Offers well appointed accommodation and great kiwi hospitality, located on a high country farm in the Waitaki Valley a short drive from Kurow.

Oamaru Motor Lodge

Offers modern motel accommodation boasting 15 superior suites and apartments, double glazed for quietness and warmth.

Oamaru Top 10 Holiday Park

Offers a range of holiday accommodation in Oamaru including motels, self contained units, cabins, tenting and powered sites.

Dome Hills Station Country Lodge

Offers luxury accomodation on a high country station in the Kakanui Mountains. Ideal for people who enjoy fishing, hunting or walks.

Moeraki Village Holiday Park

Offers self-contained motel units, tourist flats, cabins plus power and tent sites located above the boat harbour in Moeraki.

Avenue Motel

Offers well appointed motel accomodation featuring a wide range of rooms located away from the road for privacy and quietness.
