Eastland and Hawke's Bay Accommodation

To make the most of Napier's stunning waterfront, we recommend staying at Mon Logis Bed and Breakfast, Bluewater Hotel, Pebble Beach Motor Inn, the Nautilus or the Crown Hotel - all of which boast rooms with superb ocean views. In Gisborne, Portside Hotel offers superbly appointed rooms on the waters edge while Ocean Beach Motor Lodge and Pacific Harbour Motor Inn are excellent self-contained accommodation options. When visiting Hawke's Bay's stunning wine country region, we recommend staying at the luxurious Green Hill Lodge or if you are looking for Motels in Hastings, Portmans Motor Lodge and Cumberland Court Motel are highly recommended.

Napier Accommodation

Quest Apartments Napier

Offers luxury apartment accommodation in a fully refurbished Spanish Mission style building in the centre of Napier.

Bluewater Hotel

Offers luxury hotel accommodation situated in the popular inner harbour area boasting well-appointed rooms and marina views.

Pebble Beach Motor Inn

Offers centrally located motel accommodation featuring in-room spa pools, kings size beds, oceanfront balconies and free broadband internet.

The Nautilus

Offers luxury, self-contained Art Deco-inspired apartments located on the waterfront boasting panoramic ocean views plus a restaurant, bar.

Mon Logis Bed and Breakfast

Offers luxury, boutique, French-style Bed and Breakfast accommodation located on Marine Parade in central Napier.

Gisborne Accommodation

Portside Hotel

Offers luxury accommodation on the waters edge featuring full kitchens, spacious living areas, furnished balconies & an outdoor swimming pool.

Ocean Beach Motor Lodge

Offers well appointed one or two bedroom apartments boasting private courtyards, an onsite Restaurant/Bar and a beachfront location.

Pacific Harbour Motor Inn

Offers centrally located motel lodgings overlooking the Turanganui River and inner harbour marina. Choose from Studios, 1 or 2 bedroom units.

Hastings Accommodation

Green Hill Lodge

Offers super-luxury lodgings in an historic colonial farming homestead located in the heart of Hawke's Bay wine country.

Portmans Motor Lodge

Offers centrally located Hastings accommodation featuring spacious 1 & 2 bedroom units plus an outdoor swimming pool.

Cumberland Court Motel

Offers luxury, spacious motel suites featuring king size beds, spa pools, wireless Internet plus a gym and indoor heated lap pool.
