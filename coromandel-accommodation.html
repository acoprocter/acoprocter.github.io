Coromandel Accommodation

There are many accommodation options to choose from in the Coromandel. In Whitianga, Admiralty Lodge Motel offers superb apartment style lodgings with a heated swimming pool and well appointed, self contained units. Marina Park Apartments is an ideal choice if you are looking for spacious, self-contained Whitianga apartments while Beachfront Resort offers fully equipped units located on the Mercury Bay waterfront. For bed and breakfast accomodation, we recommend either Driftwood Lodge or On the Beach B & B. For quality, self-catering holiday accommodation on the scenic Auckland / Coromandel highway, look no further than Miranda Holiday Park.

Miranda Holiday Park

Offers quality holiday accommodation including apartments, chalets, backpackers, tent & motorhome sites. Tennis court & hot mineral pool.

ASURE Marina Park Apartments

Offers 1, 2 and 3 bedroom apartments featuring spacious open-plan living areas with balconies / patios, fully equipped kitchens & heated pool.

Admiralty Lodge Motel

Offers modern beachfront apartments in Whitianga featuring a heated pool, fully equipped European kitchens and tiled bathrooms.
