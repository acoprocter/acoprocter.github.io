Northland Attractions

Northland offers many memorable tourist attractions for you to choose from. A visit to the Bay of Islands is an absolute must. We recommend visiting Waitangi (near Paihia) where the Treaty of Waitangi was signed. Whether you wish to learn about the Waitangi's past, explore the grounds and buildings yourself or as part of a tour, you will find the visit most rewarding. Other attractions in Northland include Whangarei city/coastline, boat cruises, the picturesque settlement of Russell, Waipoua Forest on the west coast, beach / forest walks, scenic flights, Cape Reinga and Ninety Mile Beach (in the far north).

Fullers GreatSights Bay of Islands

Offers boat cruises / coach tours to see Dolphins, the Hole in the Rock, Cape Reinga and the famous Ninety Mile Beach.

Waipoua Forest Kauri Coast

Conservation website about the forest that is home to three quarters of New Zealand's remaining Kauri trees including Tane Mahuta.

Waitangi Treaty Grounds

Location of the signing of New Zealand's founding document, the Treaty of Waitangi. Offers tours, cultural displays & performances.

Whangarei Visitors Guide

Offers a large resource on visiting the Whangarei district including information on the main destinations, attractions & accommodation.

Bay of Islands Scenic Flights

Offers scenic fixed wing / helicopter flights throughout the picturesque Bay of Islands and further north to Cape Reinga.

Destination Northland

The official website for Destination Northland offering a comprehensive tourism / visitor attractions and accommodation guide.
