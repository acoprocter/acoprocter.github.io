Taupo Tourist Attractions

Lake Taupo offers many rewarding and memorable visitor attractions for you to choose from. The Lake offers plenty of fun activities - swimming, fishing (regarded as the best Rainbow Trout fishery in the world) and boat cruises. Another recommended attraction is to Kayak or boat to the Maori Rock Carvings at Mine Bay. Standing 10 metres tall and only accessible by boat, the carvings are trully impressive and a great way to enjoy a sunny day on the Lake. If you are looking for an exciting, action-packed adventure, we highly recommend taking a 30-minute ride with Hukafalls Jet.

Hukafalls Jet

Offers an exhilarating jet boat ride along the mighty Waikato River past the Wairakei Thermal Power station to the impressive Huka Falls.

Taupo DeBretts Spa Resort

Offers several mineral-rich, hot thermal pools to relax in. Also features a children's pool and an interactive warm water playground.

Chris Jolly Scenic Boat Cruises

Offers scenic boat cruises of Lake Taupo. See the Maori Rock Carvings up close & enjoy the views of the Tongariro National Park.

KayakingKiwi Adventures

Offers various Lake Taupo kayaking experiences including the chance to get up close to the Maori Rock Carvings.
