Bay of Plenty Tourist Attractions

The Bay of Plenty offers many exciting and memorable tourist attractions. Tauranga is a vibrant city located at the entrance to one of New Zealand's largest natural harbours. Nearby Mount Maunganui offers pristine beaches and is a popular summer holiday destination. If you are after a rewarding 45 minute stroll, we recommend walking the scenic Mauao base track. Whakatane (an attractive town set on the banks of the Whakatane River) is also well worth a visit. It is the main base for taking trips out to White Island, one of the world’s few accessible live volcanoes.

Volcanic Air Safaris

Offers flights to & landings on the active volcano of White Island. Also includes a one hour guided walk of this fascinating natural attraction.

Whale and Dolphin Watch

Offers the chance to swim with Dolphins plus view other marine wildlife such as gannets, petrels, penguins, Orca and sperm whales.

Spring Loaded Adventures

Offers unforgettable NZ adventures including Jet boating on the magnificent Kaituna River, four wheel driving, helicopter flights plus a café/garden bar.

Adventure Bay of Plenty

Offers guided sea kayaking and mountain biking tours throughout the Bay of Plenty including Mt Maunganui, Motiti Island, Rotorua Forest & more.

Kiwi 360

Celebrates the iconic Kiwifruit and the rich culture that surrounds it with orchard tours, kiwi cuisine cafe meals and a souvenir shop.

White Island Tours

Offers guided walking tours of White Island - get up close to the bright yellow sulphur crystals plus venture right up to the main crater’s edge.

Te Puke Visitors Guide

Offers an online guide to visiting Te Puke, the Kiwifruit Capital of the World. Includes info on tourist attractions, accommodation and more.

Whakatane.com

A comprehensive guide to visiting the township of Whakatane. Includes info on local attractions, events, accomodation and more.

Bay of Plenty Tourism

The Bay of Plenty's main tourism portal offering an in-depth visitor tourist attractions guide plus a large index of accommodation options.
