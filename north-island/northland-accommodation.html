Northland Accommodation

This region offers many accommodation options for you to choose from. If you are planning on visiting the beautiful Bay of Islands, we recommend staying either in Paihia, Russell or Kerikeri. Much of the accomodation in the Bay of Islands is at or near Paihia - with Russell and Kerikeri also being popular places to stay. For relaxed, comfortable budget accommodation in five locations (including Whangarei, Russell and Kerikeri), we recommend Top 10 Holiday Parks.

Copthorne Hotel & Resort

Offers premium waterfront accommodation set in 60 acres of subtropical gardens near Paihia and boasting uninterrupted Bay views.

Edgewater Palms Apartments

Offers luxurious, waterfront apartments opposite the beach featuring modern kitchens, swimming/spa pool access & panoramic views.

Scenic Hotel Bay of Islands

Located in Paihia and set in four acres of park-like surrounds and gardens. Feaures deluxe rooms that face into the garden.

Nautilus Resort

Offers luxury apartment accommodation close to Waitangi / Paihia featuring a swimming pool surrounded by gardens and palm trees.

Paihia Pacific Resort Hotel

Offers boutique hotel accomodation near Paihia. Features a swimming pool, gym, sunbed & sauna. A short walk from the ocean.

The Duke of Marlborough Hotel

Located on the Russell waterfront and offering a variety of modern, well appointed rooms to suit different budgets.

Huntaway Lodge

Offers super-luxury lodge accommodation overlooking Te Ngaere Bay. Features a balcony-deck with breath-taking ocean views.
