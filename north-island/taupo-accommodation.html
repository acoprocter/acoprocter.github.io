Taupo Accommodation

Lake Taupo offers a huge variety of accommodation options to choose from. Whether you are looking for self-contained Motels & Apartments in the central township or luxury Homestay and Lodge accomodation with commanding Lake views, you will find the ideal place to stay with NZ Escape. If you are searching for Hotels in Taupo or in the Tongariro National Park, we have a comprehensive listing of accommodation providers. We also offer many bed and breakfasts as well as boutique lodgings a short walk from the town centre.

Paeroa Lakeside Homestay

Offers luxury Taupo bed and breakfast accomodation in a peaceful setting featuring breathtaking lake views and a private beach.

Alpine Lake Motor Lodge

Offers luxury accommodation close to the shopping centre and lake. Features kitchens, under floor heating and free broadband internet.

Waimahana Apartments

Offers studio, two and three bedroom apartments with balconies overlooking a swimming pool located on the Lake Taupo waterfront.

Beechtree Suites

Offers luxury motel accommodation located close to the lake, catering for family holidays, romantic weekends and business travel.
