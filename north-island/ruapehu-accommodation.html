Whakapapa and Ohakune Accommodation

Whakapapa and Ohakune offer plenty of great accommodation options to choose from. If you are looking for a hotel in Ohakune, we recommend Powderhorn Chateau, while the best options for self-contained accomodation include either The Hobbit Motorlodge or Ossies Chalets and Apartments. Whakapapa village lodging options include the Chateau Tongariro which offers luxury accommodation, while the Skotel Alpine Resort and Whakapapa Holiday Park cater to a range of more modest budgets.

Ohakune Accommodation

Ohakune Top 10 Holiday Park

Offers a range of holiday accommodation in Ohakune including motels, self contained units, cabins, tenting and powered sites.

The Hobbit Motorlodge

Offers quality self contained and serviced lodgings in Ohakune. Features an outside spa pool, Sky TV, wireless Internet and a childrens play area.

Ossies Chalets & Apartments

Offers Scandinavian-style chalets / apartments in Ohakune featuring a sauna, modern family-friendly units with spacious rooms and superb mountain views.

Whakapapa Accommodation

Skotel Alpine Resort

Whakapapa accomodation featuring well-appointed hotel suites, budget cabins / backpacker rooms, a restaurant / bar, lounge, spa pool and sauna.

Chateau Tongariro

Offers luxury hotel and motel accommodation at the foot of Mt Ruapehu. Features restaurants, a gym, sauna, pool, tennis court, cinema and golf course.

Whakapapa Holiday Park

Offers a wide variety of accomodation options including self-contained motel units, tourist cabins, a backpacker lodge and tenting sites.
