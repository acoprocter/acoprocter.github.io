New Plymouth Accommodation

The city of New Plymouth offers a wide range of quality accommodation options. Of the Hotels in the central city, we recommend either the small luxury Nice Hotel or the larger Devon Hotel. For self-contained motels, look no further than Abode on Courtenay Motor Inn, Bella Vista Motel or Amber Court Motel - all of which offer quality accomodation and are located a short walk from the central business district. For peaceful, eco-style accommodation in the countryside, we highly recommend considering Ahu Ahu Beach Villas where you will be treated to luxury rural lodgings with magnificent sea views.

Nice Hotel, Restaurant and Suites

Located close to Pukekura Park and Coastal Walkway, this small luxury hotel offers beautifully appointed rooms, designer bathrooms plus an adjoining table restaurant.

The Devon Hotel

Offers a range of luxury, superior and business guestrooms featuring spa baths, satellite TV plus free broadband Internet access.

Ahu Ahu Beach Villas

Offers superbly appointed self-contained eco villas plus a luxury paladian styled lodge set on farmland and offering magnificent sea views. .

Abode on Courtenay Motor Inn

Located in central New Plymouth, offering modern, 4-star rated motel lodgings featuring air-conditioned units with full cooking facilities and free wireless Internet.

Bella Vista Motel

Offers modern, self-catering, studio and family motel accommodation featuring free broadband Internet and located in central New Plymouth - directly opposite Govett-Brewster Art Gallery.
