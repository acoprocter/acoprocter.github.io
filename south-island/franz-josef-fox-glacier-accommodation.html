Fox and Franz Josef Glacier Accommodation

Both Fox Glacier and Franz Josef Glaciers offer a wide variety of accommodation options to choose from. Whether you are looking for self-contained Motels / Apartments close to the town centre or luxurious Resorts with Lodge style accomodation featuring commanding mountain views, you will find the ideal place to stay with NZ Escape. If you are searching for Hotels in Franz Joseph Glacier or Fox Glacier, we have a comprehensive listing of accommodation providers. We also offer bed and breakfast accomodation as well as luxury apartment style villas a short drive from the town centre.

Westwood Lodge

Luxury bed and breakfast lodge accommodation set in a bush garden close to Franz Josef township. Boasts superb rainforest and mountain views.

Rainforest Retreat

Nestled in 6 acres of native bush in Franz Josef village, offering options suited to various budgets including Tree Houses, Tree Lodges, Tree Huts and Park Motels.

Scenic Hotel Franz Josef Glacier

Offers quality hotel accommodation with cafe and restaurant set in beautiful rainforest surroundings within Franz Josef village.

Jag Escape Franz Alpine Retreat

Offers family-friendly, luxury chalet-style accommodation boasting SKY TV, Wireless internet and superb alpine views.

Glenfern Villas

Offers luxury 1 / 2 bedroom villas in Franz Josef Glacier. Spacious, modern apartment style accommodation with fully equipped kitchens.

Sunset Motel

Offers well-appointed Motel accommodation boasting mountain views set in tranquil farmland a short walk from Fox Glacier village.
