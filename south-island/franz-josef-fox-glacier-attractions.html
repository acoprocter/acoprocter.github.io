Fox and Franz Josef Glacier Attractions

Visiting the Glaciers should be the first activity on your list. A relatively easy walk will get you up close to the terminal face of both Glaciers. For the more adventurous, guided hiking / walking on the Glaciers is well worth considering. Fox Glacier Guiding will guide you onto the ice via a secret track through bush regenerating from previous glacial advances & share with you their knowledge of the geology of the area. Franz Josef Glacier Guides will take you deep in the heart of the glacier where you can squeeze through tight blue crevasses, slide through ice caves or even ‘franz shuffle’ down near vertical walls of ice.

Fox Glacier Guiding

Offers a range of guided hiking tours / walks on to Fox Glacier catering for a variety of fitness levels. Walks vary from 2 - 9 hours in length.

Glacier Helicopters Scenic Flights

Offers scenic helicopter flights over Fox Glacier / Franz Josef Glaciers & Aoraki / Mt Cook National Park. Also offers Glacier landings.

Air Safaris Scenic Flights

Offers a 'Grand Traverse' scenic flight with incredible views of the Glaciers plus the Westland and Mount Cook National Parks.

Franz Josef Glacier Guides

Offers half day Franz Josef Glacier hiking tours where you can experience blue crevasses, Ice caves, Ice climbing and valley walks.

Franz Josef Glacier Guide

A comprehensive information guide to the Glacier's features plus the many Department of Conservation run walkways in the area.

Glacier Country Visitor Guide

Offers daily glacier updates, live webcams, visitor maps plus information on tourist attractions, activities, walks, accommodation, restaurants and cafes.
