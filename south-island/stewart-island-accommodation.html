Stewart Island Accommodation

Stewart Island offers a variety of accommodation to choose from. Kaka Retreat offers eco-retreat accommodation close to the beach. Bay Motel offers honeymoon suites plus a spacious 3 bedroom apartment. Both Stewart Island Lodge and Glendaruel Bed and Breakfast boast commanding ocean views while Rakiura Lodge offers family-friendly accomodation surrounded by native bush. For boutique lodgings also offering superb views of Halfmoon Bay, look no further than Port of Call.

Stewart Island Lodge

Offers private bed and breakfast accommodation a short walk from the village centre, featuring ensuite bathrooms, an expansive balcony and sweeping ocean views.

Bay Motel and Apartment

Offers studio, 1 or 2 bedroom units one spacious three-bedroom apartment. Boasts great views over Halfmoon Bay and frequent visits by the Kaka bird.

Port of Call

Offers boutique B&B and self-catering accommodation boasting superb views of Halfmoon bay. Surrounded by native bush, gardens and abundant bird life.

Kaka Retreat

Offers luxury superior suites plus fully self-contained cottages featuring tv's and wireless Internet. Surrounded by native bush and an abundance of bird life.

Glendaruel Bed and Breakfast

Offers private B&B accommodation boasting centrally heated rooms, a beautiful garden, superb hospitality and great views over Golden Bay.

Rakiura Lodge

Offers comfortable, relaxed self-contained lodgings featuring spacious bedrooms, a barbeque, central heating and full kitchen facilities.
