Abel Tasman Accommodation

This region offers many accommodation options for you to choose from. If you are planning on walking or kayaking the Abel Tasman, we recommend staying in Motueka or Marahau. Finding available accommodation in Marahau (which is located at the southern end of the Abel Tasman walkway) can be difficult so we therefore recommend booking well in advance. Accomodation on the walkway itself is limited to basic huts & camping grounds.

The Resurgence

Luxury Eco-Lodge in 50 acres of bush and birds. Closest 5-star lodgings to Abel Tasman. Ideal for couples who enjoy good food and nature.

Ocean View Chalets

Offers studio, one and two bedroom accommodation in Marahau. Set in a private, elevated position with superb ocean views.

Mapua Chalets

Offers 1 & 2 bedroom chalets located on a secluded ridge near Mapua. Includes satellite TV, internet & Tasman Bay views.

Tasman Village Cottage

Offers a 1-bedroom cottage set on a half acre property in beautiful garden surroundings located between Mapua & Motueka.

Awaroa Lodge

Luxury accommodation at the northern end of the Abel Tasman National Park. Nestled in native bush, two minutes from the beach.

Benvenuti Villa Kaiteriteri

Offers luxurious Italian style villa accomodation situated on the main Kaiteriteri ridge overlooking the Abel Tasman National Park.
