West Coast Accommodation

The West Coast offers accommodation to suit all budgets. In the north, we recommend Last Resort, Rough and Tumble Bush Lodge and Omau Settlers Lodge. In Punakaiki, The Punakaiki Resort, Te Nikau Retreat and the stunning Beach Hideaway cottages offer a range of options. In Greymouth, Aachen Place Motel, New River Bluegums B&B or Oak Lodge Homestay are good options. In Hokitika, Rimu Lodge, Shining Star or Annabelle Motel offer excellent lodgings.

Westport | Punakaiki | Greymouth | Hokitika

Westport Accommodation

Carters by the Sea

Offers brand-new, luxury boutique motel accommodation featuring studio suites overlooking Carters Beach with cafe/bar adjacent.

Omau Settlers Lodge

Offers well appointed motel accommodation at Cape Foulwind. Features king-size beds, crafted rimu finishings and stunning scenery.

Last Resort - Karamea

Offers fully self-contained cottages, ensuite rooms plus budget accommodation. Also features an onsite Restaurant, Cafe and Bar.

Punakaiki Accommodation

Punakaiki Resort

A modern Punakaiki beach hotel complex offering private balconies, room service, wireless Internet and superb panoramic views.

Te Nikau Retreat

Surrounded by native bush, offering ensuite rooms, self-contained cottages, budget backpacker cabins and tenting sites.

Beach Hideaway

Offers luxury, self-catering cottage accommodation boasting private, peaceful native bush surroundings and stunning panoramic sea views.

Greymouth Accommodation

Aachen Place Motel

Offers well appointed Greymouth motel lodgings offering a range of spacious standard studio and luxury family units to choose from.

Oak Lodge Homestay

Offers well-appointed bed and breakfast lodgings boasting spacious bedrooms, a tennis court, swimming pool, sauna and hot spa pool.

Hokitika Accommodation

Shining Star

Offers luxury Hokitika beachfront motel chalets, apartments, budget cabins plus powered campervan sites - all surrounded by 4 acres of native gardens.

Rimu Lodge

Offers luxury boutique lodge accommodation set in a peaceful, elevated location boasting breathtaking river and mountain views.

Annabelle Motel

Centrally located Hokitika accommodation offering well-appointed studio, 1 and 2 bedroom units with full cooking facilities plus ensuite bathrooms.
