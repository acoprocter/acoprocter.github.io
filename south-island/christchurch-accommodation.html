Christchurch Accommodation

There is a huge variety of options for accommodation in Christchurch. Whether you are looking for hotels in the central city or family-friendly holiday parks, you will find the ideal place to stay with NZ Escape. If you are searching for Apartments, see the listings of accomodation providers below. We also feature bed and breakfasts plus boutique lodges a short walk from Cathedral Square and the Arts Centre. For centrally located motels, look no further than City Centre Motel.

The Classic Villa

A renovated, Italian style historic home offering five star luxury boutique hotel accommodation on Worcester boulevard opposite the Arts Centre.

City Centre Motel

Offers centrally located, quality self-catering motel accommodation ranging from spacious studio apartments through to two bedroom apartments.

Copthorne Hotel Commodore

Offers well appointed hotel accommodation close to Christchurch airport. Boasts an indoor heated swimming pool, spa, sauna and free airport transfers.

Sudima Hotel Christchurch Airport

Hotel accommodation located 2 minutes from Christchurch airport offering a courtesy shuttle to and from the airport plus an outdoor swimming pool.

Ibis Christchurch

Offers quality 3-star hotel accommodation located beside the new "pop up" Cashell Mall, boasting 155 contemporary rooms, a restaurant and bar.

CentrePoint on Colombo

Offers quality studio, one and two bedroom motel lodgings located close to the Christchurch Casino, Convention Centre and Town Hall.

Salerno Motel Apartments

Centrally located motel accommodation offering studio, one and two bedroom units. Boasts high-speed internet, hand held phones and LCD TVs.

Ashleigh Court Motel

Offers spacious, studio, one and two bedroom units in a quiet location near Mona Vale Gardens, Riccarton Bush and a short walk from Hagley Park.
