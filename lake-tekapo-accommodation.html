Lake Tekapo Accommodation

Lake Tekapo offers a variety of accommodation options. For quality, centrally located, self-contained accomodation, we recommend Lake Tekapo Scenic Resort which offers a range of Motel lodgings. A short walk from the town centre, the Lake Tekapo Holiday Park offers everything from budget camping sites, backpackers through to tourist cabins and motels. At the higher end of the market, The Godley Hotel and Moonlight bed & breakfast both offer premium quality accommodation.

Lake Tekapo Scenic Resort

Offers self-contained motel accommodation in a central location featuring wireless Internet access and views over Lake Tekapo and the surrounding mountains.

Lakeview Tekapo

Offers modern, luxurious, self-contained accommodation boasting uninterrupted views of Lake Tekapo and the Southern Alps.

Peppers Bluewater Resort

Offers luxury studio, one, two and three bedroom villas as well as an onsite Restaurant - located a short walk from the shops.

Tekapo Heights

Offers well-appointed Tekapo Bed & Breakfast accommodation plus a self-contained holiday home boasting modern, spacious living and superb lake views.

Three Rivers Lodge

Lake Tekapo Bed and breakfast accommodation boasting superb lake views, heated floors, flat screen TVs and free wireless internet.

Lake Tekapo Motels and Holiday Park

Set amongst mature trees on the shores of Lake Tekapo and offering tenting / camping sites, tourist cabins, motel units and backpacker lodgings.

Lake Tekapo Holiday Homes

Offers a comprehensive index of holiday houses / homes plus bed and breakfast options in and around the picturesque Lake Tekapo township.

Moonlight Bed and Breakfast

Offers luxury bed and breakfast accommodation featuring well appointed rooms, panoramic Lake Tekapo / Mountain views plus warm, friendly hospitality.
