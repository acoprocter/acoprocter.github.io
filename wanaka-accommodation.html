Wanaka Accommodation

The resort town of Wanaka offers a huge variety of accommodation options. Whether you are looking for Hotels, Apartments, Lodges with commanding mountain / lake views or self-contained motels or holiday cottages near the town centre, you will find the ideal place to stay here. We also list a number of apartments as well as several bed and breakfasts. There are also a number of holiday homes and houses available to rent in Wanaka.

McDougall's Apartment

Offers quality Apartment accommodation featuring two bedrooms, wireless Broadband Internet access, Satelite TV and a drying room.

Minaret Lodge

Offers luxury 5 star Wanaka bed and breakfast lodgings in a 2 acre park-like setting. Located close to Lake Wanaka and the town centre.

Wanaka Edge Apartments

Offers well appointed, self-contained, 1 / 2 bedroom apartment suites featuring spa / sauna facilities, breakfast hampers and ski lockers.

Peak-Sportchalet

Offers quality, self-contained studio, one and two bedroom chalet accommodation in a quiet location with picturesque mountain views.

Wanaka Heights

Centrally located motel accommodation featuring spacious units with private balconies, all boasting panoramic mountain and lake views.

Maple Lodge Wanaka

Offers boutique, Bed and Breakfast accommodation set in its own 7-acre private grounds with a plantation of sugar maple trees.

Wanaka Cottages

Offers luxury holiday houses for couples, families and large groups. Includes a private, sunny deck with cedar spa pool plus a modern kitchen.
