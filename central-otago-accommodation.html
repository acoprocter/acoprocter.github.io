Central Otago Accommodation

Central Otago offers a huge variety of accommodation options to choose from and caters for all budgets. While there are plenty of motels in Alexandra and Cromwell, we recommend trying the hosted options on offer. Quail Rock B&B and Hawkdun Rise offer privacy and relaxation set in superb locations. If you are looking for a warm, quiet motel in Alexandra, look no further than Almond Court Motel. While there are many great options to choose from on the Rail Trail, the Lodge and Cottages Wedderburn is well worth considering.

Hawkdun Rise Bed & Breakfast

Offers private, boutique B&B lodgings set on a vineyard near Alexandra. Features Queen size bed rooms, ensuites and a verandah.

Golden Gate Lodge

Centrally located Cromwell Hotel accommodation offering a restaurant, two bars plus couple, family and disability-friendly units.

Quail Rock Bed and Breakfast

Offers well-appointed bed & breakfast accommodation boasting an extensive garden and superb views from the hills above Alexandra township.

The Lodge & Cottages Wedderburn

Offers both lodge accommodation (a refurbished farm homestead) plus separate, self-contained cottages located at the mid-point of the Otago Central Rail Trail.

Almond Court Motel

Offers self-contained, centrally heated, quiet accommodation in Alexandra. Boasts a swimming pool, wireless internet and more.
