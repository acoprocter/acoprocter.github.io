Blenheim Accommodation

While staying in Blenheim, if it is centrally located hotel accommodation you are looking for, we recommend Chateau Marlborough. A short drive out of town, the Vintners Hotel offers luxurious lodgings in a vineyard setting. To make the most of the stunning countryside and superb kiwi hospitality the area has to offer, then your best options are Stonehaven Homestay or Blue Ridge Estate. St Leonards Vineyard Cottages offer self-contained accomodation in lovingly restored historic farm buildings on a peaceful rural property with stunning views. For family holiday accommodation in a central location, we recommend Top 10 Blenheim Holiday Park.

Chateau Marlborough

Offers luxury 5 star hotel accommodation located in central Blenheim featuring a restaurant, conference facilities, a swimming pool with poolside bar and Complimentary WIFI.

Stonehaven Homestay

Nestled within a 20 acre sauvignon blanc vineyard, offering ensuite bedrooms, a large private garden, swimming pool, full cooked breakfast and genuine ‘Kiwi’ hospitality.

Blue Ridge Estate

Luxury bed and breakfast lodgings set among 20 acres of grape vines and garden, featuring spacious guestrooms boasting stunning views to the Richmond Range.

St Leonards Vineyard Cottages

Offers private, self-contained accommodation in "lovingly" restored traditional farm buildings featuring modern facilites, great views and a peaceful country setting.

Marlborough Vintners Hotel

Offers well-appointed Blenheim hotel accommodation featuring a bar, restaurant and luxury one bedroom suites with room service plus stunning vineyard views.
