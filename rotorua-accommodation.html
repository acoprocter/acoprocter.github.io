Rotorua Accommodation

Rotorua offers a huge variety of accommodation options to choose from. For Hotels in Rotorua, we have plenty of accomodation providers on offer. For private, luxury lodge accommodation boasting superb lake views, look no further than Koura Lodge. Ann's Volcanic Motel is another excellent choice for quality motel lodgings. We also recommend Wai Ora Lakeside Spa resort for anyone looking for well-appointed, Hotel-style, boutique accommodation in Rotorua. For families looking for quality holiday accomodation, we recommend All Seasons Holiday Park.

Koura Lodge

Set on the shores of Lake Rotorua, offering luxury accommodation featuring private ensuites, a lakeside spa / sauna, Tennis Court and panoramic lake views.

Holiday Inn Rotorua

Located beside the Whakarewarewa Thermal Reserve, offering modern guest suites boasting city and lake views. Also features a Restaurant, Bar and conference facilities.

Wai Ora Lakeside Spa Resort

Located on the shores of Lake Rotorua, offering multi-award-winning luxury, hotel-style accommodation with 24-hour room-service, restaurant / bar and Day Spa.

All Seasons Holiday Park

Offers a wide range of quality self catering accommodation to suit all budgets, boasting a heated swimming pool, tennis court and children's playground.

Sudima Hotel Lake Rotorua

Set on the shores of Lake Rotorua, offering well-appointed guest rooms with private balconies, a heated swimming pool, private spas and wireless Internet access.

Novotel Lakeside Rotorua

Located on the shores of Lake Rotorua, offering spacious Rotorua Hotel accommodation with modern ammenities. Also features an onsite fitness centre and spa.

Millennium Hotel Rotorua

Located close to Rotorua Museum, offering well-appointed lakefront accommodation featuring a Restaurant, heated swimming pool, day spa with sauna and gymnasium.
