Blenheim Attractions

A tour of Blenheim's many world-class vineyards and wineries is a must. Wine Tours by Bike offers self-guided tours to most of the popular vineyards - hire a bike for a few hours or the whole day. Of the fully guided tour options available, we recommend either Explore Marlborough or Marlborough Wine Tours (who also offer boat cruises in the Marlborough Sounds). Of the many vineyards Blenheim has to offer, we recommend visiting Highfield Estate Winery where you can enjoy fine wine with great food in their in-house Restaurant. Other tourist attractions include Driftwood Eco-tours, which offers tours for bird-lovers to the Wairau Lagoon.

Wine Tours By Bike

Offers fun, self guided, scenic wine tours by bike that caters to the rider's ability and needs. Packages include bicycle hire, a personalised tour route and map.

Driftwood Eco-tours

Offers fully guided tours of the Wairau Lagoon - home to more than 90 different kinds of birds including the Royal Spoonbill, Black Fronted Tern and Bar-tailed Godwit.

Marlborough Wine Tours

Offers a variety of short, medium and all-day personalised tours of the region's vineyards and wineries plus an optional boat-cruise in the Marlborough Sounds.

Highfield Estate Winery

A boutique Marlborough winery situated on the Brookby Ridge, open daily, offering award winning wines, a restaurant and magnificent views of the Wairau Valley.

Explore Marlborough

Offers a selection of guided tours (by bike or car) of Marlborough's wineries and boutique food producers. Also offers a combination of food, wine, art and garden tours.

Destination Marlborough

The official online tourism guide to visiting Marlborough including information on the area's history, tourist attractions, accommodation, transport options and more.
