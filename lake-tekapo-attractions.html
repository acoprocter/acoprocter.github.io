Lake Tekapo Attractions

Picturesque Lake Tekapo and the Mackenzie country area offers many exciting, memorable tourist attractions. We highly recommend taking a scenic flight with Air Safaris. They will take you over the majestic southern alps where you can view New Zealand's highest mountain, Mt Cook. Earth and Sky offer day-time tours and also night-time tours where you can star-gaze through New Zealand's largest telescope. Roundhill ski area offers great skiing and superb alping views in the winter.

Air Safaris

Offers spectacular, scenic fixed-wing flights over Lake Tekapo, Mackenzie country, Mt Cook and the alpine wonderland of New Zealand's majestic Southern Alps.

Tekapo Springs

Offers three landscaped Hot Pools (varying in temperature from 36 - 40 degrees celcius) set amongst native alpine vegetation, featuring superb lake and mountain views.

Earth and Sky

Offers fascinating, informative daytime guided tours around Mt John Observatory as well as nighttime stargazing tours at Mount John and Cowan's Observatory.

Mackenzie Alpine Horse Trekking

Offers a variety of enjoyable half, full or multi-day guided horse treks throughout the spectacular Mackenzie country including Mt John and further in to "Glacier Country".

Lake Tekapo Conservation Guide

Department of Conservation guide to visiting Lake Tekapo and the Mackenzie Country. Includes information on the features, history, geology, activities and accommodation.

Roundhill Ski Area

The closest ski field to Lake Tekapo, offering great family-friendly skiing on terrain suitable for the beginner to advanced skier.

Lake Tekapo Tourism Guide

The official online tourism guide to visiting Lake Tekapo including information on tourist attractions, accommodation, transport options, local events and more.
