Aoraki / Mt Cook Accommodation

The Aoraki Mount Cook region offers many accommodation options for you to choose from. The Hermitage offers everything from self-contained, family Motel / Chalet accomodation to Luxury Hotel rooms boasting spectacular views of Mt Cook. Budget accommodation is available at Alpine Lodge and YHA Mt Cook. Nearby White Horse Hill camping ground is another option for campers. Glentanner Park, a few minutes drive from Mt Cook village, offers a range of holiday park accommodation.

The Hermitage Hotel

Offers Hotel, Motel and Chalet accommodation with many of the rooms boasting spectacular mountain views. Also offers a cafe, lounge and two restaurants.

Glentanner Park

Offers a range of holiday accommodation including camping (and powered) sites, cabins and self-contained motels. Located a short drive from Mt Cook village.

Aoraki Mt Cook Alpine Lodge

Offers mid-range holiday lodgings in the Mt Cook Village centre. Features ensuite bathrooms, a fully equipped kitchen and lounge.

YHA Mount Cook

Offers budget backpacker hostel accommodation featuring spacious rooms, central heating, a cosy fire, sauna, BBQ and picnic area.

White Horse Hill Campground

Offers 50 campervan and tenting sites set amongst superb alpine scenery near Mt Cook village. Facilities include toilets, picnic tables and a shelter.

Mountaineering Huts

Department of Conservation administered huts (NOTE: For mountaineers only - climbing skills are needed to reach these huts).
