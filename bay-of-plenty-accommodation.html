Bay of Plenty Accommodation

The Bay of Plenty offers many accommodation options for you to choose from. If you are looking quality hotel accommodation in central Tauranga, we recommend Hotel on Devonport. We have a selection of luxury apartments located on or near the main beach in Mt Maunganui. For family holiday accomodation near The Mount, we also list a camping ground option. Ohiwa holiday park (20 minutes from Whakatane) is a great option for anyone interested in budget holiday lodgings near the beach.

Oceanside Resort and Twin Towers

Luxury serviced apartment accommodation close to Mount Maunganui, overlooking main beach. Options range from executive studio guestrooms through to 3 bedroom penthouse apartments.

Hotel on Devonport

Offers luxury, boutique style hotel accommodation in downtown Tauranga. Boasts well appointed rooms with balconies, secure undercover parking and high speed internet access.

Cutterscove Apartments

Offers luxury apartments in Mount Maunganui. Features designer kitchens, open plan living plus free access to an outdoor lagoon / pool area.

Hilltop Apartment

Offers luxury Tauranga apartment accommodation in a restored historic homestead boasting superb views of the harbour and Mt Maunganui.

Cosy Corner Holiday Park

Offers a range of holiday accommodation options in Mt Maunganui including camping sites, budget cabins & self-contained family units.

Ohiwa Holiday Park

Located near Opotiki, offering Ohope Beach accommodation boasting beach-front campsites, ocean views plus spacious grounds with native flora.
