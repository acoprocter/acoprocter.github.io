Kaikoura Accommodation

There are a many excellent accommodation options in Kaikoura to choose from. For anyone looking for premium quality accomodation outside the township, Hapuku Lodge and Endeavour Heights bed and breakfast offer private, superior lodgings. For centrally located accommodation, we recommend either White Morph Motor Inn or Anchor Inn Motel - both are located on the waterfront. If you are on a more modest budget, the Top 10 holiday park (near the centre of town) and Kaikoura Cottage Motels (located on the outskirts) are both worth considering.

The White Morph

Offers quality self-contained apartment accommodation featuring 5-star spa rooms that boast commanding full views of the ocean and kaikoura mountains.

Hapuku Lodge and Tree Houses

Luxury accommodation, featuring Tree Houses nested 10m above the ground in a canopy of native Manuka trees, boasting panoramic ocean and mountain views.

Anchor Inn Motel

Modern motel accommodation located on the Kaikoura waterfront, boasting well appointed units plus superb views of the Pacific Ocean and Kaikoura ranges.

Kaikoura Cottage Motels

Offers modern, self contained, fully-insulated cottages set in a quiet rural location close to Kaikoura. Also offers a children's playground, BBQ and free WiFi.

The Fairways at Ocean Ridge

Offers luxuriously appointed studio, one and two bedroom apartments located beside the Kaikoura Golf course. Also offers an exclusive in-house catering menu.

Endeavour Heights B&B

Offers super-luxury Kaikoura accommodation featuring gourmet continental breakfasts plus rooms that boast private, seaward-facing balconies and panoramic views.
