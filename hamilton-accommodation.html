Hamilton Accommodation

Hamilton offers a huge variety of accommodation options. For quality, centrally located Hotels in Hamilton, we recommend the Novotel Tainui Hotel or Hotel Ibis - both overlook the Waikato river and are close to the central business district. Other well located options include the Ashwood Manor Motel, Park View Motor Lodge, Bella Vista Motel and the Ventura Inn. If you have an early flight or want to stay near the Mystery Creek events centre, Hamilton Airport Motor Inn is the perfect choice.

Novotel Tainui Hotel

Located on the banks of the Waikato in the city centre, offering well appointed rooms, a restaurant, lobby bar plus a gym, sauna, spa and massage therapies.

Ibis Hamilton Tainui Hotel

Offers well appointed, modern guest rooms boasting great city or river views. Centrally located beside the Waikato river and near cafes, restaurants and Skycity casino.

Park View Motor Lodge

Offers spacious, tastefully decorated rooms, an indoor heated swimming pool and jacuzzi, located near Waikato Stadium and close to the city centre.

Hamilton Airport Hotel

Located next to the Hamilton International Airport Terminal and the Mystery Creek Events Centre. Offers studio, executive or apartment units.

Ventura Inn and Suites

Offers centrally located motel apartment lodgings featuring king-size beds, spa baths, a swimming pool, fitness room and a complimentary continental buffet breakfast.

Ashwood Manor

The closest motel to downtown Hamilton, offering modern, peaceful Inner-city accommodation featuring well presented rooms (some with spa baths) and free wireless internet.

Waitomo Accommodation

Waitomo Top 10 Holiday Park

Located in central Waitomo Caves Village, offering a range of accommodation including Ensuite motel units, cabins, powered campervan / caravan sites and tenting sites.
