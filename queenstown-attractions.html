Queenstown Attractions

Queenstown offers a variety of exciting and memorable tourist attractions. Top of your must-do list should be Skyline Gondola which will hoist you to 800 metres on nearby Bob's Peak. Not only can you take in the stunning scenery, you can also enjoy other activities such as the Luge Ride, Bungy Jumping and Paragliding. A visit to Queenstown wouldn't be complete without seeing the Kawarau Bridge AJ Hackett Bungy Jumping. Other attractions include the Steamship "TSS Earnslaw", Jet Boating with Shotover Jet,Scenic Helicopter flights, Glenorchy, the historic mining town of Arrowtown plus the Hollyford and Routeburn walking tracks.

TSS Earnslaw Boat Cruises

Offers scenic cruises on Lake Wakatipu and to Walter Peak Station aboard the gracious 1912 vintage steamship TSS Earnslaw.

Shotover Jet

Offers a high-speed, exhilarating Jet Boat ride through the spectacular canyons of the Shotover River near Queenstown.

Heliworks Queenstown Helicopters

Offers scenic helicopter flights, heli-weddings plus exclusive charters to Milford Sound, Fiordland, Mt Cook and Lord of the Rings filming locations.

Hollyford Track

A 3-day guided walking track offering spectacular scenery including snowy mountains, pristine rivers, lakes, native rainforest and remote coastal vistas.

Dart River Jet Safaris

Offers a unique, memorable combination of Jetboating and "FunYak" inflatable canoeing experiences deep in the pristine Dart River Valley.

Discover Arrowtown

An historic mining town, Arrowtown offers a relaxed atmosphere, stunning natural surroundings and lies along banks of the Arrow river.

AJ Hackett Bungy Jumping

Offers a range of locations and heights to Jump from including the Kawarau Bridge Bungy - home to the original 43-metre Bungy Jump.

Glenorchy

A spectacular 45 minute drive from Queenstown, Glenorchy is known as the Gateway to Paradise and the famous Routeburn walking track.

Routeburn Track

A world-renowned hiking track that traverses 32 km of Mount Aspiring and Fiordland National Parks in NZ's South West World Heritage Area.

NZONE Skydiving

Offers exhilarating skydiving jumps from 15,000 feet above the stunning backdrop of Queenstown / Lake Wakatipu. Free courtesy coach.
