Twizel Accommodation

Twizel offers a range of accommodation options. For quality motel lodgings, we recommend the Mountain Chalet Motels and Aspen Court Motel. Mackenzie Country Hotel offers well-appointed lodgings and is a good option if you require an in-house restaurant. Of the hosted accommodation near Twizel, we recommend Omahau Downs. In Omarama, look no further than the Top 10 holiday park.

Mountain Chalet Motels

Offers one and two bedroom chalet-style motels in Twizel featuring kitchens, satellite TV, queen size beds, heat pumps and air-conditioning.

Mackenzie Country Hotel

Featuring natural stonework and offering well appointed Hotel accommodation located in central Twizel. Rooms feature private balconies with mountain views.

Omahau Downs

Located on an historic high country sheep station. Offers ensuite lodge and cottage accommodation boasting magnificent mountain views.

Omarama Top 10 Holiday Park

Offers a range of holiday accommodation in Omarama including motels, self contained units, cabins, tenting and powered campervan sites.

Aspen Court Motel

Offers well appointed motel accommodation in central Twizel, featuring air conditioned rooms, king size beds, full kitchen facilities and underfloor-heated bathrooms.

High Country Lodge & Backpackers

Offers a range of centrally located lodgings set in sunny, spacious grounds. Features include self-catering kitchens, BBQ's and wireless internet.
