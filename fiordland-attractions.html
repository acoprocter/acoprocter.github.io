Fiordland Attractions

In Fiordland, Milford Sound is the most popular destination, offering tourist attractions such as boat cruises and kayaking trips that explore the outer reaches of the National Park. Experiencing Fiordland by boat is most rewarding and will make for an unforgetable day out. Doubful Sound, offering equally stunning scenery and diverse wildlife, is another highly recommended destination. Real Journeys offers day and overnight boat cruises where you can experience the beauty of this spectacular part of New Zealand.

Real Journeys Milford Sound

Offers a range of daytime and overnight scenic boat cruises on modern, spacious vessels. Queenstown Coach or flight connections also available.

Rosco's Milford Kayaks

Offers a range of all-year-round, fully guided sea kayaking eco-adventures that showcases Milford Sound in its own unique kiwi way.

Doubtful Sound Boat Cruises

Offers a variety of daytime and overnight boat cruises in picturesque Doubtful Sound. Boasts stunning scenery and diverse wildlife encounters.

Milford Track

An informative conservation guide to New Zealand's most famous walkway including it's features, track description and accommodation options.

Fiordland Conservation Guide

A comprehensive visitor guide to the Fiordland National Park including information about it's features, activites and campsite / hut lodgings.

Mitre Peak Cruises

Offers Milford Sound boat cruises with Mitre Peak and Stirling Falls views. Dolphins, penguins and seals are often spotted during the trips.

Fiordland Wilderness Experiences

Offers guided kayaking trips throughout fiordland including Milford Sound, Doubtful Sound, Lake Manapouri and Lake Te Anau.

Fiordland Tourism Guide

Destination Fiordland's official website featuring a wealth of visitor information. Includes information on transport, activities and accommodation.
