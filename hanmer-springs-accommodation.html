Hanmer Springs Accommodation

Hanmer offers a plentiful variety of accommodation options to choose from. Whether you are looking for luxury serviced apartments in Hanmer Springs or budget camping grounds, you will find the ideal place to stay with NZ Escape. For those of you searching for Hanmer Springs Motels, we have a comprehensive listing of accomodation providers below. If you are looking to rent a holiday house, we also list websites that offer great rental Homes in Hanmer.

Hanmer Holiday Homes

Offers the largest range of short and long term self-contained, fully furnished holiday houses for rent in the Hanmer Springs area.

The Chalets Motel

Offers 10 self-contained, separate timber chalets set in a quiet, sunny location boasting a large children's play area and excellent views of the surrounding mountains.

Aspen Lodge Motel

Offers spacious, centrally located motels boasting LCD TV's, Sky TV, 3 seater couches, fully equipped kitchens, complimentary WiFi Internet and DVD movies.

Heritage Hanmer Springs

Offers a range of quality accommodation from hotel rooms to 3-bedroom villas, an outdoor swimming pool, set amongst mature trees and boasting superb alpine views.

Settlers Motel

Offers luxury motel accomodation set in a peaceful, sunny location featuring well appointed one or two bedroom suites with heat pumps, air-conditioning and free wifi.

Village Lake Apartments

Offers luxury multi-bedroom apartments boasting king-size beds, designer kitchens, double spa baths and broadband Internet. Located a short walk from the hot pools.

Hanmer Inn Motel

Offers quality, comfortable motel accommodation featuring studio, one and two bedroom units, located just two minutes walk from Hanmer Springs Thermal Reserve.
