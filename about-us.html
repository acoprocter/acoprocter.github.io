About Us

NZ Escape is a 100% New Zealand owned and operated company located in the coastal township of Timaru. The business was set up in May of 2000 by Hamish Kirke - originally as a hobby website. It has since grown from humble beginnings into a thriving family-owned business.

Our website offers a comprehensive New Zealand accommodation and tourism attractions resource and is used by thousands of travel planners in 120+ countries worldwide - every week. As well as providing a top-notch Tourism marketing service, we also offer web site design, development and hosting.
