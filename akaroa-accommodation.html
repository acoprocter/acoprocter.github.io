Akaroa Accommodation

The beautiful settlement of Akaroa offers many accomodation options. Finding available accommodation in Akaroa during the busy summer months can be difficult so we therefore recommend booking well in advance to ensure that you can stay at your preferred lodgings. If you are looking for good kiwi hospitality in a relaxing, peaceful location, we recommend staying at Paua Bay Farmstay. Akaroa cottages is another option for those looking for private lodgings with superb views.

Akaroa Criterion Motel

Offers spacious Apartment-style motel accommodation boasting individual balconies, double-glazed windows, under-floor heating and spectacular harbour views.

Akaroa Cottages

Offers luxurious, private cottage accommodation set on 12 acres of native bush land boasting superb panoramic views of the harbour and sea.

Akaroa on the Beach

Offers self-contained, waterfront architecturally designed apartments boasting luxurious ensuites, located in a private setting with spectacular sea views.

Paua Bay Farmstay

Offers charming farmstay accommodation boasting an outdoor bath, peaceful location with spectacular coastal scenery and warm kiwi hospitality.

Akaroa Village Inn

Apartment style lodgings with private balconies & modern amenities located on the waterfront in the central Akaroa shopping area.

Akaroa Waterfront Motels

Offers spacious, comfortable, fully self-contained 1, 2 and 3 bedroom units (refurbished) located in a spacious garden setting with spectacular views over the harbour.

Tresori Motor Lodge

Offers modern studio, one and two bedroom units boasting fully equipped kitchens, spacious living areas, large spa baths, broadband internet plus a central location.

Bruce Waterfront Apartments

Offers self contained apartment accomodation, a waterfront location with sea views in central Akaroa.
